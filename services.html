Private capital financings

Capital aligned with the business plan.

We advise on capital structure and source private debt, mezzanine, preferred equity, and growth equity from institutional investors. The process includes financing strategy, materials, investor outreach, term comparison, diligence, and documentation.

  • Growth capital and acquisition financing
  • Private equity and minority investment
  • Senior, subordinated, and mezzanine debt
  • Pre-IPO positioning and public financing support

Capital is more than price.

We evaluate dilution, control, covenants, flexibility, investor fit, and long-term strategic implications alongside headline economics.
